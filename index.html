It gives us immense satisfaction and pleasure to announce the release of the first edition of our annual magazine 'Milepost, Making them count!'. It is an attempt to provide a new forum for interaction among the civil community. Our sole goal was to produce an excellent magazine that reflects the breadth and vitality of our department.

Civil and Environmental Engineering are among the most premier and fast evolving branches of science and engineering. They provide a vast scope for research and a plethora of opportunities for the budding engineers to contribute to the society and make it better place to live by innovations in research. The Association of Civil Engineers (ACE), IIT Patna (@iit_patna ), is proud to announce the ‘Engineers’ Conclave’ in the maiden edition of Samnivesha.We, the Association of Civil Engineers are excited to release Samnivesha. Samnivesha is going to be the annual Technical fest of the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, IIT Patna henceforth. After almost a year in making, our team is proud to announce the first ever edition of Samnivesha. Two days of events, workshops and discussion , all related to the field of Civil Engineering. We promise our maiden edition will certainly be an event to remember and we sure hope to see you there!! The conclave will act as a platform for aspiring engineers and researchers to present their research prospects which will be reviewed by intense interaction and questionnaire with leading researchers and professionals For more details, please visit samnivesha.org #Samnivesha #Feb_09_10
